About Premium Estates

25 years of excellence in luxury real estate

Our Story

Founded in 1999, Premium Estates has established itself as the premier luxury real estate agency in the region. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide unparalleled service and expertise to clients seeking exceptional properties.

Over the past two decades, we have grown from a small boutique agency to a market leader, handling some of the most prestigious transactions in the luxury property market. Our success is built on trust, integrity, and an unwavering commitment to our clients.

Today, our team of over 50 dedicated professionals continues to set the standard for excellence in real estate, combining cutting-edge technology with personalized service to deliver results that exceed expectations.
